Work With Us

We offer custom insights and strategy to help organizations navigate the complex kidney health landscape.

Co-Led by Paul Grill, Signals Group works alongside companies across the product lifecycle, from innovation to commercialization.

Paul Grill is a career management consultant, M&A advisor, and venture investor. His most recent role was the Launch Director for the National Kidney Foundation’s (“NKF’s”) Innovation Fund where he led investments in start-ups bringing innovative treatments to kidney patients. Paul is also a living kidney donor and former NKF Board Member who is passionate about improving the lives of kidney patients.

From market research and landscape analysis to investment support, financial modeling, and expert feedback, we deliver actionable insights that help companies make confident decisions and accelerate impact.
